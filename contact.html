Our Story

BreezyBite Bakery is a culinary haven situated in the heart of Kandy town, celebrated for its commitment to crafting freshly baked goods using locally sourced, premium ingredients. Our diverse range includes an array of bread, pastries, and more. With branches strategically located in Peradeniya, Katugastota, Gampola, Akurana, Nawalapitiya, and Kadugannawa, we aspire to bring the best quality products and exceptional service closer to our customers.

Our devoted team ensures that every order is delivered punctually and handled with the utmost care. Immerse yourself in the joy of freshly baked goods from BreezyBite Bakery today!

Adam Smith

Owner, BreezyBite
